Research

My research focuses on low-dimensional materials and their applications in energy harvesting, energy storage, biosensing, and spintronics. I work extensively with MXenes, Kagome lattice materials, and boron-based materials, employing computational and theoretical approaches to understand and predict novel material properties. My goal is to bridge fundamental physics with real-world technological applications in next-generation devices.
